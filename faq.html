Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you wanted to know about SPX engines, tuning, and our race-ready F1 style kart.

Engines & Builds

What engine sizes do you offer?

We currently offer three main displacements: 198cc, 212cc, and 224cc. Each has multiple trim levels with different internal components, airflow, and exhaust options.

Are these engines hand-built?

Yes. Every SPX engine is assembled by hand, with upgraded components and clearances checked before it leaves our hands.

Do you dyno test the engines?

Our racing engines are test-run and tuned. Premium builds include dyno-tested power figures with a printout of the results.

Is horsepower guaranteed?

Horsepower numbers are estimates based on our testing. Actual power can vary depending on fuel, elevation, weather, and the rest of your setup.

Support & Warranty

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer startup and setup guidance, plus general tuning advice. Once the engine is running and modified, responsibility for maintenance and operation is on the owner.

Do you offer a warranty?

Due to the racing nature of these engines, we offer very limited warranty coverage. Any disassembly, internal modification, or abuse will void the warranty immediately.
