Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about SPX engines, tuning, and our F1-style kart.

Engines & Builds

What engine sizes do you offer?

We currently offer three displacements: 198cc, 212cc, and 224cc. Each comes in multiple trims with different internals, airflow, and exhaust.

Are the engines hand-built?

Yes. Every SPX engine is assembled by hand with upgraded components and checked before it leaves our shop.

Do you dyno test the engines?

Our premium builds are dyno-tested during development. Some packages include an optional dyno sheet with estimated horsepower.

Is horsepower guaranteed?

Horsepower figures are estimates based on our testing. Real-world numbers can vary with fuel, elevation, weather, and your setup.
