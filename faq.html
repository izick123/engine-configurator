Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about SPX engines, tuning, and our F1-style kart.

Engines & Builds

What engine sizes do you offer?

We currently offer three main displacements: 198cc, 212cc, and 224cc. Each platform has multiple trim levels with different internal parts, airflow, and exhaust options.

Are the engines hand-built?

Yes. Every SPX engine is hand-assembled with upgraded components. Clearances and critical fasteners are checked before it leaves our hands.

Do you dyno test the engines?

Our higher trim engines are dyno-tested during development. Production racing builds are test-run and tuned; some packages include a dyno sheet with estimated horsepower.

Is horsepower guaranteed?

Horsepower numbers are estimates based on our testing. Actual power can vary with fuel, elevation, weather, and the rest of your setup. Always treat printed numbers as estimates, not guarantees.
